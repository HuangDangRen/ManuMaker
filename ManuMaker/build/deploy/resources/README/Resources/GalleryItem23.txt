§ANNODRAFT[
§To log critical information during a process that requires use of your hands, you can record an audio draft of a steps documentation.  To start recording, click the button Record Audio Draft (1) and speak into your default microphone. When you're done, click the Stop Recording button(2), and the Audio clip will be attached to the current slide.   To replay the audio draft, you can use the Audio Player (3) available in the Draft and Refine tabs.   The Audio Draft is a tool exclusively intended for the documenter, and is hence not available in the View tab. All relevant information from the Audio draft should be transcribed to text during the refinement-phase of the documentation (as of ManuMaker Version 1.0, this has to be done manually). You can store ONE audio clip per slide. If you wish to overwrite the existing one, just click on (1) again and confirm that you wish to discard the existing audio clip.
