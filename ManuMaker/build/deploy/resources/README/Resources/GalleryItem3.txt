The size-limit of the subject is mainly set by the documenters workspace configuration and the length of the webcam cable. ManuMaker has been created mainly with devices in mind which can be placed on a desktop or workbench equipped with a PC and e.g. a desk lamp on which the camera can be mounted for steady and precise positioning.&nbsp;</font></li><li><font face="Segoe UI">Software (using a screen capture tool)</font></li><li><font face="Segoe UI">Systems which consist of hardware as well as software, such as a company's requirements, ManuMaker can still serve as an organisational tool for drafting the documentation.&nbsp;<br>In case of hardware-documentation done with a digital camera and an office-grade word processor the following steps are eliminated when using ManuMaker:&nbsp;</font></li><ul><li><font face="Segoe UI">Operating the camera by hand</font></li><li><font face="Segoe UI">Organizing the facilitate their work (especially test stands and protoypes, which may not receive a complicated documentation due to shortage of time).&nbsp;<br><br></font></li><li><font face="Segoe UI"><b>Entrepreneurs</b> who need to provide documentation for the products they manufacture or import and distribute .<br><br></font></li><li><font face="Segoe UI"><b>Students</b>, especially in practically-oriented subjects of the&nbsp;S.T.E.A.M.-variety who need to document their contributions to intra- or extra-curricular projects where the basic functions of their work needs to be
