§Adding a Title Slide
§31
]TEXTFIELDS§
§TAGS[
§project title slide; project title page; add slide; insert chapter- or title slide; refine mode; refine tab
]TAGS§
§ANNODRAFT[
§To add a title slide for the documentation click on Insert Chapter- or Title Slide (1). Klick Select...(2) and then Title Slide(3) and confirm with OK(4).   The title slide information can be set via Settings->Project Settings. An image can be loaded to be displayed on the title slide (e.g. company logo).  After modifying the Project Settings, the title slide is automatically updated.
