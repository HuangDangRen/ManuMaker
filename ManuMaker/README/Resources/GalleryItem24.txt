§To remove the current slide or any of its external components such as the Picture or the Audio Draft (text is internal, as it can simply be deleted in the editor), click on the button Remove (1).   A dialog will show where you can chose which component to delete: -> The complete current slide with all its text, images, audio... -> The current image, in case the slide shall be text-only -> The audio draft  NOTICE:  Components are permanently removed. There is no mechanism to get them back.
